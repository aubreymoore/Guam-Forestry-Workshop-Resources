## Flame Tree Looper Outbreak on Saipan
- On November 2, 2016, Arnold Route, Northern Marianas College, sent me images of a caterpillar defoliating flame trees. - Arnold had correctly identified the caterpillar as flame tree looper, *Pericyma cruegeri*
Flame tree looper was recorded on Rota in 1973 and Saipan in 1974 ([iNaturalist](http://www.inaturalist.org/observations?locale=en&order_by=observed_on&place_id=any&subview=table&taxon_id=324331&user_id=aubreymoore&verifiable=any))
